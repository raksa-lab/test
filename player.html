Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Portugal, is one of football’s all-time greats. He has won 5 Ballon d'Or awards and played for top clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Ronaldo is also Portugal’s all-time leading scorer.

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Argentina, is one of football’s greatest players. He has won 7 Ballon d'Or awards, 4 Champions League titles, and the 2022 FIFA World Cup. Messi has played for Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain, and Inter Miami.
