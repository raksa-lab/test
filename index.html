Team Spain

Spain is a Southern European country known for its rich culture and history. It has a constitutional monarchy, with King Felipe VI as the head of state. The national football team has won the World Cup 2010 and Euro 2008 and 2012.

Team Bazil

Brazil is the largest country in South America, famous for its culture and landmarks like Christ the Redeemer. The national football team has won the FIFA World Cup five times. Brazil is a constitutional republic, with Jair Bolsonaro as president.

Team Germany

Germany is a central European country known for its history, culture, and strong economy. The national football team has won the FIFA World Cup four times. Germany is a federal republic, with Olaf Scholz as chancellor.

Team Real

Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is a top football club based in Madrid, Spain. The club has won multiple La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles. It is known for iconic players like Cristiano Ronaldo and Sergio Ramos.

Team Cambodia

The Cambodia national football team, created in 1933, represents Cambodia in international football and is governed by the Football Federation of Cambodia (FFC), competing in various regional and global tournaments.

Team Mancity

Manchester City Football Club, founded in 1880, is a top-tier English football team. Competing in the Premier League, the club has won several domestic titles. Pep Guardiola has managed the team since 2016.
